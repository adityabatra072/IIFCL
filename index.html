Internship Overview

During my 4-week Information Technology internship at IIFCL, I've been focused on gaining a comprehensive understanding of their core IT systems and operations. Weekly Progress

Week 1: Market Analysis Infographic

Developed a single-page application (SPA) infographic for IIFCL market analysis. This project leveraged HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Chart.js to deliver responsive, canvas-based data visualizations and structured HTML/CSS diagrams.
